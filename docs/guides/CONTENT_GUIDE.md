## ðŸŽ® Game Tips

### Ship Progression
1. Start with **Frigate** (any race)
2. Progress to **Destroyer** for more firepower
3. Train **Cruiser** for versatility and survivability
4. Specialize based on preferred playstyle

### Fitting Basics
- **Frigates**: Focus on speed and agility
- **Destroyers**: Maximize turret DPS, fit for glass cannon
- **Cruisers**: Balanced tank and damage, or specialized roles

### Damage Types
- **EM**: Amarr lasers, effective vs shields
- **Thermal**: Gallente blasters, drones
- **Kinetic**: Caldari missiles, Gallente railguns
- **Explosive**: Minmatar projectiles, missiles

### Resistance Profiles
- **Caldari/Guristas**: Strong kinetic shield resistance
- **Gallente**: Strong thermal armor resistance
- **Amarr**: Strong EM armor resistance
- **Minmatar**: Strong explosive armor resistance

### Drone Usage
- Light drones: Anti-frigate
- Medium drones: Anti-cruiser, general purpose
- Heavy drones: Anti-battleship, slow tracking
